Warranty

Goods have warranty conditions, in accordance with the current legislation of the Republic of Moldova and commercial policies of manufacturers. Goods are delivered in original packaging obtained from suppliers;
You will receive a warranty card containing the terms of use, transportation and storage, as well as information about service centers that perform warranty repairs;
The conditions for providing and canceling the warranty may differ from manufacturer to manufacturer, and are clearly indicated in the product warranty card;
Within 14 days from the date of purchase, if the goods turned out to be faulty, you need to come to our service center;
Please be understanding that the presence of viruses or illiterately installed programs on computers and laptops is not a malfunction of the device, and the problem can be fixed by our specialists for a fee. We recommend that you purchase anti-virus and other programs along with your hardware;
After the expiration of 14 days from the date of purchase of the defective product, you must contact the authorized service center of the manufacturer for troubleshooting;
Other products that do not have a manufacturer's service center warranty are serviced at the service center of our store. In our service center, we try to minimize the terms of repair or exchange of your equipment. In cases where the malfunction is obvious, there are no suspicions of hidden damage and improper operation of the product, and the repair of the product is impossible, we change the product immediately after the diagnosis.

Return item

Please note that all products sold in our store belong to the category of "technically complex products" that are not subject to unconditional return or exchange if for some reason they did not suit the buyer and at the same time do not have flaws;
Based on common sense, we are ready to meet you further than the law requires. In many cases, you can return or exchange parts with which you have an incompatibility issue by filing an online claim.
